Beyond the boarders of our home world, in the vast expanse that lays ahead of us, our delicate physiology must endure new and unknown challenges. The long-term survival of our species will require ingenuity and courage. Our will is restrained only by our biological limitations.

To ensure the best medical care we will preemptively treat illness with precision medicine. To prepare for unknown biological reponses we will predict genotypic and phenotypic assocciations before they naturally occur. To embark on the final frontire, we shall adapt our tools to develop the next generation of technology for transportation and biological safety.

"We wish to pursue the truth no matter where it leads - but to find the truth, we need imagination and skepticism both. We will not be afraid to speculate, but we will be careful to distinguish speculation from fact." - Carl Sagan.

These challenges are addressed through our publicly-funded academic research, international collaboration, and invitation-only genomic analysis. With minimalism and privacy in mind, this site is built to be light and fast as well as containing no ads, trackers, cookies, &c. This site is under live development; some placeholder pages are included for testing.
